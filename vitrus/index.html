What is Coronavirus?

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus.

The best way to prevent and slow down transmission is be well informed about the COVID-19 virus, the disease it causes and how it spreads.

GOOD PRACTICES

Protect yourself better

Social Distancing

To practice social or physical distancing stay at least 6 feet (about 2 arms’ length) from other people.

Wash regularly

As the virus can survive on surfaces of different materials for at least 2-3 days, surfaces potentially contaminated with COVID-19 virus should be sanitised.

Go for checkups

Maybe your nose is a little runny, your throat is feeling a bit tight and scratchy, and you’re starting to feel some body aches, and perhaps even a fever.
